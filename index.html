Amigurumi

If you ever wanted to make your own stuffed animals, learning how to crochet is a good way to start.

Here are some things you need to get you started on your invasion of stuffed animals:

  • Crochet hooks
  • Yarn
  • Stitch marker
  • Yarn needle

Crochet Basic Terms

Abbreviation Crochet Stitch
ch chain
dc double crochet
dec decrease
FO finish off
hdc half double crochet
rep repeat
sc single crochet
sk skip
sl st slip stitch
st stitch
tog together
tr triple crochet
yo yarn over
